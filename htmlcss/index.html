ORGANIZATION BACKGROUND

By having the sense of reponsibility and sympathy towards the orphans, a group of social activist and Selangor State Government senior officer have successfully built and registered "Pertubuhan Pendidikan Anak-Anak Yatim Selangor" under the Registration Number ARS/Sel/50/66 on August 16, 1966.


After the approval of the registration, the appointed Committee members have been actively working in order to achieve the purpose of the establishment. Upon the contribution by YBhg. Allahyarham Dato' Haji Harun Idris and wife YBhg. Datin Salmah Suleiman, Selangor State Government has granted a land in the Hulu Kelang district (HS.231) covering 4.8 acres by the approval document number PHT.G.2/139/75/ In order to define the project that has been successfully implemented by YBhg. Allahyarham Dato' Haji Harun Idris, YBhg. has been appointed as the chairman of the organization.


From the result of their hard work, several programs have been held such as Dinner Gathering, Gala Premiere, as well as plea to the committee members, the Organization managed to build a Rumah Bakti building efficaciously at the cost of RM158,107.81. DYMM Almarhum Sultan Salahuddin Abdul Aziz Shah Alhaj ibni Almarhum Sultan Hisamuddin Alam Shah, theSultan of Selangor has given a royal approval to become RBHK patron.


At the initial stage, the orphans' intake was in 1976 which are 26 residents that consist of 13 males and 13 females which ranges from age 6 untill 12 years old. RBHK was officially inaugurated by DYMM Sultan Selangor on 5 March 1977. From year to year, the number of orphans have increased and up untill 2017, Rumah Bakti was occupied by 66 orphans aged 7 untill 17 years old.
